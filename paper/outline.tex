\begin{abstract}

% Problem: mechanical models of snow stability require elastic properties not directly
% measured in the field. Ideally, these properties could be estimated from field observations.
% There are a number of proposed parameterizations for mechanical properties from field observations,
% but no systematic framework to apply, enumerate, and compare them.

% Approach: Method implementation of selected parameterizations. Parameterization graph and
